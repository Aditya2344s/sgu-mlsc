About MLSC

The Machine Learning Student Club (MLSC) at Sanjay Ghodawat University is a vibrant community of students passionate about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. We aim to foster learning, innovation, and collaboration in the exciting field of AI and ML.

Our club organizes workshops, hackathons, guest lectures, and projects to help students gain practical experience and stay updated with the latest trends in the industry.
